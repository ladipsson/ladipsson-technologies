Our History

Ladipsson Technologies - started operation from Lagos, Nigeria in 2022 but was registered with CAC in 2021. The company started with web designing and graphic design as the major service before going into other services like Mobile APP development, UI/UX design, Custom CRM/Software development, and SEO services. The agency is a child idea of Mr. Shadrach Arowolo, shortly after completing his Web Development Trainings, borne out of a passion for designing and coding, Shadrach started Ladipsson Technologies from the comfort of his one-room apartment before going commercial.
